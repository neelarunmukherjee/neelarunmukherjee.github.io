About me

A Small Introduction About Me

Neelarun Mukherjee

I come from Kolkata, the capital city of West Bengal in India also known as a the City of Joy . Currently I am in my final year of studies at Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur , one of the top universities in India. I am currently completing my Integrated Masters on Exploration Geophysics from the Department of Geology and Geophysics. My primary research interest lies in modeling transport and flows in the subsurface. How to address the growing drinking water crisis due to groundwater contamination around the world by developing robust models to explain transport phenomena in the subsurface, primarily in the vadose and the critical zones considering the effects of increasing human interference? How to overcome the hurdle to simulate the reactive transport of injected CO2 over long periods in saline aquifers considering the factors of interfacial tension, hysteric relative permeability, and dynamicchanges in salinity? These are some of the questions that motivate me to choose the field of computational hydrogeology as my primary research interest.

I have worked with some international laboratories along with some projects in my university during my undergraduate life. During my third year, I have worked in Geosciences Rennes , a CNRS lab in the small beautiful city of Rennes in France. During my fourth year I have worked in Sorbonne University , Paris. I also worked at the University of Alberta after getting selected for the UARE(University of Alberta Research Experience) Scholarship. Scroll down to see projects done in this field. Welcome to contact me and discuss any interesting topics with me!

Other than studies, I like photography and traveling. I like to travel to places and take photos of life on street. Have a look at my instagram to check out some of my clicks. Other than that, I like to play the violin sometimes and read novels. I also like cars and racing. In my college years, I was a part of Formula SAE Team, TeamKART from IIT Kharagpur. Currently I enjoy watching F1 on the weekends and am a supporter of Max Verstappen from the Red Bull F1 Team.
